## Блок 1: Что такое микрофибра

| Тезис | Источник (#) | Описание / Цитата |
|-------|--------------|------------------|
| **Микрофибра замена натуральной замше** | 23, 27 | Synthetic polyester/polyamide blend заменила chamois leather в 2000-х. Microfiber безопаснее (не drags dirt), более absorbent, многофункциональная. Chamois — pushes water (squeegee-like), microfiber — absorbs & traps dirt |
| **Почему микрофибра безопаснее** | 7, 12, 22, 23 | Ultra-fine synthetic fibers (<1 микрон) trap dirt INSIDE towel, не drag contamination ACROSS paint. Chamois drags grit (scratches). Microfiber: 7-10x weight absorption, pulls water/dirt away |
| **Состав 80/20 vs 70/30** | 2, 5, 8, 34 | Both = polyester/polyamide ratio. **70/30:** More polyamide = softer, more absorbent, expensive, professional-grade (top detailers use). **80/20:** More polyester = durable, easier drying, better value, heavy-duty. For paint: 70/30 preferred |
| **Электростатический заряд** | 17, 40 | Microfiber split fibers create electrostatic charge that attracts dust/dirt. Fabric softener BLOCKS this charge (catastrophic) |

## Блок 6: Правильный уход (Стирка)

### Перед стиркой

| Этап | Действие | Почему | Источник (#) |
|------|---------|--------|------------|
| **Встряхивание (pre-shake)** | Shake each towel to remove loose dirt | Reduce abrasive particles в wash | 15, 16 |
| **Pre-wash treatment** | Для heavy residue: APC (all-purpose cleaner) или d-Limonene citrus | Breaks up oils, waxes, solvents перед main wash | 19 |
| **Segregation** | Wash interior/wheel/polish towels SEPARATELY | Cross-contamination prevention | 14, 19 |

### Стирка

| Параметр | Рекомендация | Почему | Источник (#) |
|----------|--------------|--------|------------|
| **Detergent** | Dedicated microfiber wash (free-detergent, no scent, no dyes) | Microfiber-specific formulation, не clogs fibers | 15, 17, 18, 28 |
| **Temperature** | Warm (65-85°F / 18-29°C), НИКОГДА hot (>104°F) | Hot water damages synthetic polymer structure, causes fiber separation | 16, 17 |
| **Wash cycle** | Gentle или microfiber-safe cycle (НЕ heavy/normal) | Aggressive agitation loosens fibers | 15, 16, 40 |
| **Fabric softener** | ❌ НИКОГДА! | Blocks electrostatic charge (permanent damage to function) | 17, 40 |
| **Bleach** | ❌ НИКОГДА! | Damages fibers, causes discoloration | 15, 17, 18 |
| **Quantity detergent** | Minimal (less than normal laundry) | Excess detergent clogs fibers | 15, 18 |

### Сушка

| Параметр | Рекомендация | Почему | Источник (#) |

## Блок 7: Критические ошибки

| Ошибка | Последствие | Как правильно | Источник (#) |
|--------|-----------|---|------------|

### Противоречивые данные:

- **Chamois absorption:** Some sources claim 10x weight (wet pickup), others say 5x. Lab test (source 21) settled at 5x synthetic vs 8x microfiber
- **Lifespan:** Ranges 50-500 washes (depends heavily on care). 80/20 longer than 70/30 if washed correctly
- **Temperature threshold:** Some allow 104°F (hot), others say no higher than 85°F (warm). Consensus: cool/warm only safe

### Практические замечания:

1. **GSM = базовый metric** — 1000+ для сушки, 300-400 для glass, 500+ для полировки
2. **70/30 vs 80/20** — Professional 70/30, Budget 80/20. Разница очевидна в softness/durability
3. **Edgeless = not optional для ЛКП** — 20% reduction micro-scratches proven
4. **Color-coding MANDATORY** — Cross-contamination (wheels → paint) = disaster
5. **Detergent = critical** — Fabric softener = permanent damage, только microfiber-specific
6. **Temperature + Technique** — Cool water + straight-line motion = 80% prevention of marring
7. **Chamois = outdated** — Microfiber 8x better absorption, much safer, multi-task capable
8. **Pre-wash новых towels MANDATORY** — Residual manufacturing chemicals/lint
